Scholarly is the fun, safe way for young children to learn to read. Designed by educators for ages 3-7, our app combines proven phonics methods with interactive storytelling to build reading confidence.

PHONICS FOREST
Explore a magical forest where each tree teaches a new letter sound. Children progress through carefully sequenced phonics lessons with:
- Letter recognition and formation
- Phoneme blending and segmenting
- Sight word practice
- CVC word building
- Decodable reading passages

STORY GARDEN
Watch reading skills bloom in our interactive story garden:
- AI-generated stories that adapt to your child's reading level
- Touch-to-hear word pronunciation
- Comprehension questions woven into the narrative
- Beautiful illustrations that bring stories to life
- A growing library of age-appropriate tales

DESIGNED FOR LITTLE LEARNERS
- Large, colourful touch targets designed for small fingers
- Encouraging audio feedback and haptic rewards
- No text-heavy menus — children navigate independently
- Gentle pacing that respects each child's learning speed

SAFE & PRIVATE (COPPA COMPLIANT)
- No ads — ever
- No social features or messaging
- No personal data collected from children
- No third-party tracking or analytics SDKs
- Parental gate protects all settings and purchases
- All content reviewed for age-appropriateness

FOR PARENTS
- Track your child's phonics progress
- See which letters and words they've mastered
- Set daily reading time reminders
- Manage up to 3 child profiles (Scholar plan)
- Detailed progress reports emailed weekly
