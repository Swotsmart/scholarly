**The Unified Learning Nexus**

## Executive Summary

Scholarly is a comprehensive AI-powered education platform designed to serve the complete educational ecosystem—from early childhood through professional development. Built with Australian education standards at its core while supporting international frameworks, Scholarly integrates learning management, adaptive intelligence, curriculum alignment, scheduling, compliance, and community features into a unified experience.

**Platform Statistics:**
- 120+ specialized services
- 40+ user-facing pages
- 7 supported curriculum frameworks
- 30+ language learning options
- Full LTI 1.3, OneRoster 1.2, Ed-Fi interoperability
