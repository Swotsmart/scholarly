How It Works

Beyond quiz scores ‚Äî holistic personalization

The engine creates a multi-faceted learner profile that includes academic performance, engagement patterns, time-on-task metrics, and the curiosity signals that reveal emerging passions.

Academic Data

Traditional grades and assessment performance form the foundation, but the engine looks deeper ‚Äî at patterns within answers, time spent on different question types, and conceptual gaps revealed by specific errors.

Engagement Metrics

Time spent on tasks, interaction patterns with content, assignment completion rates, and platform navigation behaviours reveal how a student actually engages with learning material.

Curiosity-Driven Data

The questions students ask the AI Buddy, the topics they explore independently, and the conceptual rabbit holes they follow. This data is a powerful leading indicator of intrinsic motivation ‚Äî information traditional metrics miss entirely.

Adaptive Difficulty

If a student masters a concept quickly, the system accelerates to more challenging material. If they struggle, it provides remedial support, alternative explanations, or different content modalities.

Modality Switching

Automatically switches between text, visual simulations, video, and interactive exercises based on what works best for each learner. A student who struggles with text-based algebra might thrive with visual representations.

Talent Discovery

The curiosity data layer transforms the AI from a reactive tutor into a proactive talent scout ‚Äî identifying nascent interests and suggesting cross-disciplinary projects the student might not have discovered otherwise.
